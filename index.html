Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Transparent answers to the questions every property buyer, seller, and investor asks us most.

How do I start looking for a property with Pro·Buds? +

Simply reach out via our contact form, call our hotline, or visit our Makati office. A dedicated buddy-agent will schedule a free, no obligation consultation to understand your needs, budget, and lifestyle preferences. We'll then curate a shortlist of matched properties and arrange viewing schedulesall at no cost to buyers.

Does Pro·Buds charge service fees for buyers? +

Absolutely not. Our services are completely free for property buyers. We earn through standard brokerage commissions paid by developers and property sellers, as stipulated under the Real Estate Service Act (R.A. 9646).

What areas and regions does Pro·Buds cover? +

We currently operate across Metro Manila (all 16 cities and municipalities), CALABARZON (Cavite: Imus, Tanza, General Trias, Naic, Dasmariñas; Laguna: Santa Rosa, Biñan, Cabuyao; Batangas: Lipa City), and Western Visayas (Iloilo City, Pavia, Sta. Barbara, Leganes). We are actively expanding to Cebu, Davao, and Clark in the next 18 months.
