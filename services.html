The Process

How It Works

From your first message to your first night in your new home here's exactly what to expect when you work with Pro·Buds.

Step 01

Free Discovery Call

We start with a 30-minute no-obligation call where we get to know you your budget, lifestyle, timeline, and goals. No pitch, no pressure. Just a conversation between friends.

Step 02

Property Matching & Tours

Based on your profile, your dedicated buddy-agent curates a shortlist of best-fit properties and arranges virtual or on-site tours at your schedule including weekends and evenings.

Step 03

Offer, Financing & Reservation

Once you've found your match, we handle the offer negotiation, guide you through financing options, and facilitate your reservation often within 48 hours of your final decision.

Step 04

Documentation & Closing

Our docs team takes over gathering requirements, filing with BIR, coordinating with the Registry of Deeds, and getting your title transferred. You receive real-time updates every step of the way.

Step 05

Key Handover & Aftercare

On handover day, your buddy is right there with you. And our relationship doesn't end there annual property check-ins, rental assistance, and market updates are yours for life.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start looking for a property with Pro·Buds? +

Simply reach out via our contact form, call our hotline, or visit our Makati office. A dedicated buddy-agent will schedule a free, no obligation consultation to understand your needs, budget, and lifestyle preferences. We'll then curate a shortlist of matched properties and arrange viewing schedulesall at no cost to buyers.

Does Pro·Buds charge service fees for buyers? +

Absolutely not. Our services are completely free for property buyers. We earn through standard brokerage commissions paid by developers and property sellers, as stipulated under the Real Estate Service Act (R.A. 9646).

Can Pro·Buds assist with housing loans and financing? +

Yes and this is one of our most valued services. We are accredited loan facilitators for BPI, BDO, Metrobank, Security Bank, RCBC, and the Pag-IBIG Fund. We prepare bank-ready packages, help you compute your loanable amount based on income, and guide you through the process.

What areas and regions does Pro·Buds cover? +

We currently operate across Metro Manila (all 16 cities and municipalities), CALABARZON (Cavite: Imus, Tanza, General Trias, Naic, Dasmariñas; Laguna: Santa Rosa, Biñan, Cabuyao; Batangas: Lipa City), and Western Visayas (Iloilo City, Pavia, Sta. Barbara, Leganes). We are actively expanding to Cebu, Davao, and Clark in the next 18 months.

How long does the entire property purchase process take? +

For pre-selling properties, the reservation-to-title timeline typically spans 3–5 years (construction + title processing). For ready-for-occupancy (RFO) properties using bank financing, expect 45–90 days from loan application to move-in. Cash transactions can close in as little as 15–30 days. Our documentation team expedites BIR clearance, Registry of Deeds processing, and title transfer to minimize delays.

What is the minimum budget to start investing in real estate? +

With pre-selling properties, you can reserve a unit for as low as ₱5,000–₱20,000 and pay equity in low monthly installments over 24–36 months before bank financing kicks in. Our most affordable listings start at ₱1.2M in Cavite with monthly amortizations of ₱4,500–₱8,000 through Pag-IBIG. There's genuinely an entry point for every budget our agents specialize in finding the right fit for your financial situation.

Can OFWs and overseas Filipinos buy property through Pro·Buds? +

Absolutely. In fact, OFW investors are among our most satisfied clients. We offer fully digital transaction support e-signatures, video call consultations, online reservation forms, and overseas document apostilling guidance. We partner with Philippine consulates to assist with SPA (Special Power of Attorney) preparation. Your beneficiary in the Philippines can also act on your behalf with proper documentation. We've completed hundreds of OFW transactions from the US, UK, Middle East, Canada, and Singapore.
