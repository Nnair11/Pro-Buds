Philosophy

The Buddy-First
Way of Doing Business

In 1998, our founder Carlo Medrano saw a gap in Philippine real estate: agents who sold property but never truly served people. He left a prestigious brokerage firm and launched Pro·Buds from a small office in Imus, Cavite with nothing but a list of 12 clients and a radical idea: treat every buyer like your closest friend.

That philosophy grew into a company with over 300 licensed buddy-agents covering 18 provinces. The name changed. The office moved. But the core never wavered.

01.

Radical Transparency

We share every detail hidden costs, long-term projections, even market downturn risks. You deserve the full picture, not just the glossy brochure.

02.

Hyperlocal Knowledge

Our buddies live where they sell. They know which barangay floods, which street has the best school access, and where the next commercial hub is rising.

03.

Lifetime Relationship

We don't close deals and disappear. Our post-sale support team, property managers, and annual check-ins keep us in your corner long after you've moved in.

Our Journey

27 Years of Trust

1998

The First Office

Carlo Medrano opens a 12-sqm office in Imus, Cavite, with a borrowed desk, a landline phone, and the conviction that real estate agents should act like trusted friends not just salespeople. First 12 clients become lifelong advocates.

2003

PRC Accreditation & Expansion

Pro·Buds earns full PRC and HLURB accreditation. The team grows to 40 agents. The Cavite network expands to Bacoor, Dasmariñas, and General Trias, and the first partnership with Ayala Land Premier is inked.

2010

10,000 Families Milestone

A landmark year: Pro·Buds serves its 10,000th family. Coverage expands to Laguna, Batangas, and Metro Manila. The OFW investment program launches, allowing overseas Filipinos to buy property remotely with a dedicated buddy agent.

2018

Digital Transformation

Pro·Buds launches its digital platform virtual property tours, e-signature reservations, and online loan tracking. In one year, 35% of all transactions are initiated online. Named one of PropTech Philippines' top innovators.

2026

64,000 Families & Growing

Today, Pro·Buds operates across 18 provinces with 300+ agents and a growing tech team. We've served 64,000 families and are on track to reach 100,000 by 2030 While staying true to the buddy-first promise that started it all.
